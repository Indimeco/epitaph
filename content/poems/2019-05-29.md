# over the well

over the well
dusk or dawn auroras show
the foot paths worn in grass

the waters turn unreflecting
in the lidless shape, wet,
each greyed brick begets
none walk here

for acre on acre
no wind tosses up stale grain to dust
only an uncertain sky
over the well
