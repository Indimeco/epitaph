Uncut for but a month the grass
erupted out of hedge and beds
and fused itself into a mass!
Uncut for but a month the grass
extruded spindly hordes of heads!
Who knew how quickly time could pass?
I've forgot what I did instead.
Uncut for but a month the grass
erupted out of hedge and beds...
